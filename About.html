About Climate Change

Climate Change Matters For Everyone

What/Who is it Affecting?

Global security, human health, and the environment are all seriously threatened by climate change, which makes it crucial. It causes habitat loss, extreme weather, sea level rise, rising temperatures, and ecological disruption, which affects biodiversity, economies, and the security of food and water globally.Reducing the effects and intensity of climate change is yet possible. Researchers are discovering new issues, and we already know a lot of the problems and their fixes. Climate change is changing are weather patterns drastically and if humans dont take action on being greener we might not have seasons anymore.

Individuals may help by making better decisions about where they receive their energy, how they travel, and what they consume. However, the most important method for everyone to help stop climate change is to act together. This means lobbying governments and corporations to modify their policies and business practices.
